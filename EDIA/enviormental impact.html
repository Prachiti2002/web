EFFECT OF RELIABLE PUBLIC TRANSPORT SYSTEM ON ENVIORMENT

THE CONCEPT

You can soon walk past an automatic fare collection in buses and let your small chip or card pay for you .Through our project we are planning to introduce a ticket payment where a NFC(near field communication) tags whould be scanned by a ticket reader
When the passenger walks into the bus it through indoor it will automatically detect the card even if it is in pocket or bag.It detects it and registers the code of that chip.As the commuter exits a station ,the ticket reader will again detect the card and deduct the fare on distance travelled.There will be station codes,distance and ticket far

The chip can be recharged like our data pack of our phone.And its very cheap and easy proccess

The main domain of our project is to save time of passenger with ticket collector.And in view towards smart city there will be less transction proccess and also paper saving..!
